⚠️ Illegal & Unethical Access — Why NOT to Use Sci-Hub

Sci-Hub and similar websites distribute copyrighted material without authorization. While they may offer free access, their use constitutes copyright infringement and violates academic integrity.

  • Violates international and institutional copyright laws.
  • Poses legal, ethical, and cybersecurity risks to users and institutions.
  • Undermines fair scholarly publishing and open-access efforts.
  • May host malware or phishing attempts compromising personal data.

✅ Safer Legal Alternatives

  • Unpaywall and Open Access Button
  • Shodhganga and NDLI
  • CORE, AgEcon Search, and SSRN

✉️ Polite Email Template for Requesting Papers

Subject: Request for a Copy of Your Paper “[Paper Title]”

Dear Dr. [Author’s Last Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I am a researcher/student at [Your Institution].
I came across your paper titled “[Full Paper Title]” published in [Journal Name, Year],
but unfortunately, I could not access the full text through my institution.

If permissible, could you kindly share a copy (PDF or preprint version) for my academic use?
I assure you it will be used solely for research and citation purposes.

Thank you very much for your time and contribution to this field.

Warm regards,
[Your Full Name]
[Institution / Department]
[Email Address]

⚠️ Always use open-access or author-approved copies. Avoid piracy — it risks your credibility and the integrity of scholarly communication.
