🇮🇳 Legal Indian & Global Repositories

  • NDLI: National Digital Library of India (IIT Kharagpur).
  • Shodhganga: UGC–INFLIBNET repository for Indian PhD theses.
  • Unpaywall & CORE: Locate legal OA copies.
  • Author Requests (Email): Many authors will share preprints on request.

⚠️ Why NOT to Use Sci-Hub

Sci-Hub violates international copyright laws, poses legal and cybersecurity risks, and undermines scholarly publishing. Instead, use ethical alternatives or request directly from authors.

Subject: Request for a Copy of Your Paper “[Paper Title]”

Dear Dr. [Author’s Last Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I am a researcher at [Institution]. I came across your paper titled “[Full Paper Title]” published in [Journal], but unfortunately, I could not access the full text.

If permissible, could you kindly share a copy (PDF or preprint) for my academic use? I assure you it will be used solely for research and citation purposes.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
